More about me

I consider myself a passionate about technology since I was a child. My first contact with informatic systems was when I was about 4 years old. My father taught me what was a computer and what we could do with it.
So I learned with a Commodore 64 (that still works!) and a PC with MS-DOS. I remember that my dad wrote in a sheet of paper some commands to run games, so I just had to simply type them and start playing. I had fun very much with games like Prince of Persia, Wolfenstein, Doom, Duke Nukem and Arkanoid, but I think my favourites were Megaman, Crystal Caves and Mario Bros.

As for my education I went to "Escuela de Educación Técnica Fundación Fangio" secondary school, I studied English at "Instituto Cambridge", and I'm currently studying Web & Mobile Development at "Universidad Nacional de La Matanza".
I'm also learning in a virtual course of Udemy called "Master en PHP, SQL, POO, MVC, Laravel, Symfony, WordPress".

I like being in constant learning. I firmly believe that life is about that, living to learn, sharing experiences and enrich yourself to be better people and professionals every day. I always find myself predisposed to join interesting projects that promote creativity and ingenuity. I like to travel, photography, board games and football, and in my free time I like listening to new music, read, watch videos, movies and series.
